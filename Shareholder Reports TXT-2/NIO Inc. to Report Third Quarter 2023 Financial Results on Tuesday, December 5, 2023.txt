11/21/2023

About NIO Inc.
NIO Inc. is a pioneer and a leading company in the premium smart electric vehicle market. Founded in November 2014, NIO’s
mission is to shape a joyful lifestyle. NIO aims to build a community starting with smart electric vehicles to share joy and grow
together with users. NIO designs, develops, jointly manufactures and sells premium smart electric vehicles, driving innovations in
next-generation technologies in autonomous driving, digital technologies, electric powertrains and batteries. NIO differentiates itself
through its continuous technological breakthroughs and innovations, such as its industry-leading battery swapping technologies,
Battery as a Service, or BaaS, as well as its proprietary autonomous driving technologies and Autonomous Driving as a Service,
or ADaaS. NIO’s product portfolio consists of the ES8, a six-seater smart electric flagship SUV, the ES7 (or the EL7), a mid-large
five-seater smart electric SUV, the ES6 (or the EL6), a five-seater all-round smart electric SUV, the EC7, a five-seater smart
electric flagship coupe SUV, the EC6, a five-seater smart electric coupe SUV, the ET7, a smart electric flagship sedan, the ET5, a
mid-size smart electric sedan, and the ET5T, a smart electric tourer.
